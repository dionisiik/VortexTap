# VortexTap 🌀

A modern circle rhythm tapping game for iOS. Test your reflexes and build massive combos in three unique game modes!

## 📱 About The App

VortexTap is an addictive rhythm-based mobile game where players tap the screen when a moving point hits the target zone. Inspired by rhythm games like osu!, VortexTap features beautiful gradients, smooth animations, and progressively challenging gameplay.

### What Makes It Special

- **3 Unique Game Modes** - Each with distinct mechanics and challenges
- **Dynamic Targets** - Target zones that change position to keep you on your toes
- **Orbital Mechanics** - Switch between concentric circles in the innovative Orbital Mode
- **Achievement System** - 19 unlockable achievements across 5 categories
- **Dual Themes** - Choose between Vortex (purple) and Pulse (pink) themes
- **Haptic Feedback** - Feel every tap with customizable vibration effects
- **Beautiful Design** - Modern UI with gradients, glows, and smooth animations

## 🎮 Game Modes

### 🎵 Rhythm Mode
**Time Challenge - 60 Seconds**

Race against the clock in this intense 60-second challenge! The target zone randomly appears in 8 different positions around the circle after each successful hit.

**Features:**
- ⏱️ Circular countdown timer
- 🎯 8 dynamic target positions (0°, 45°, 90°, 135°, 180°, 225°, 270°, 315°)
- 🔄 Target repositions after each hit
- 💯 Score: 10 × combo per hit
- 📊 Accuracy tracking
- 🔥 Build combos for higher scores

**Strategy:**
- Watch where the target moves
- Time your taps perfectly
- Build long combos to maximize score
- Stay focused as targets jump around

---

### ♾️ Endless Mode
**Survival Challenge - Last as Long as You Can**

Survive as long as possible with only 3 lives! The game progressively speeds up based on your combo, testing your limits.

**Features:**
- ❤️ 3 lives system
- ⚡ Progressive speed increase (up to 2.5x)
- 💰 Score multiplier (up to x5 based on combo)
- 🎯 Static target position (top of circle)
- 📈 Speed indicator
- 🔥 Risk/reward gameplay

**Strategy:**
- Conserve your lives
- Build combos for higher multipliers
- Adapt to increasing speed
- Balance risk and accuracy

---

### 🌀 Orbital Mode
**Dynamic Challenge - 45 Seconds**

The most challenging mode! Both the moving point and target zone can be on any of 4 concentric orbits. Switch orbits using buttons to match the target.

**Features:**
- 🔵 4 orbital rings (different radii)
- 🕹️ Manual orbit switching (INNER ⬇️ / OUTER ⬆️)
- 🎯 Target randomly changes orbit AND position
- 💯 Score: 15 × combo per hit (highest reward!)
- ⏱️ 45-second timer
- 🧠 Requires multitasking and quick decisions

**Strategy:**
- Track both position AND orbit
- Switch orbits quickly
- Anticipate target movements
- Master the hardest challenge

## 🎯 Gameplay Mechanics

### How to Play

1. **Launch the app** - Complete the onboarding (first time only)
2. **Tap START** - Access mode selection
3. **Choose a mode** - Rhythm, Endless, or Orbital
4. **Play the game:**
   - Watch the moving point rotate around the circle
   - Tap the screen when the point enters the target zone
   - Build combos by hitting consecutive targets
5. **View results** - See your score, accuracy, and unlocked achievements
6. **Play again** or return home

### Scoring System

**Rhythm Mode:**
- Base: 10 points per hit
- Multiplier: × combo count
- Example: Combo 50 = 500 points per hit

**Endless Mode:**
- Base: 10 points per hit
- Multiplier: 1x to 5x (based on combo milestones)
- Example: Combo 50+ = 5x = 50 points per hit

**Orbital Mode:**
- Base: 15 points per hit (highest!)
- Multiplier: × combo count
- Requires matching both orbit and position

### Tips for High Scores

- 🎯 **Build combos** - Never miss to maximize multipliers
- 👁️ **Watch the target** - Especially in Rhythm and Orbital modes
- ⏱️ **Manage time** - Stay calm in the final seconds
- 🔄 **Practice orbital switching** - Master the hardest mode
- 💯 **Aim for accuracy** - Higher accuracy = better achievements
